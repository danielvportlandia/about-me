Hi, My name is Daniel and welcome to my page.

I was born in Portland, Oregon but raised near the Flying M Ranch in rural Yamhill County. The Flying M Ranch was known for their rustic lodging, horseback trails, and their airstrip where pilots would fly in from all around for Sunday brunch.

I am a proud father of a sweet little girl named Cassandra who may have siblings one day. I come from a relatively large family with having 6 siblings and a decent handful of nieces and nephews. My family is pretty evenly spread throughout Washington and Oregon.

I have worked in the medical field most of my life starting off as a nursing assistant, then a patient services coordinator, then medical billing specialist, and most recently as a medical coding specialist.

I am currently attending Code Fellows at the Seattle campus.
